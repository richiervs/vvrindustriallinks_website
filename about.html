About Us

VVR Industrial Links was founded in November, 1989 by RTR Velmurugan. The first unit was established at soorampatti, Virudhunagar.

Over the last 27 years we have organically grown from 50K units per month initially to 4.25L units per month of production capacity today with great effort and highly disciplined manufacturing process.

Our experience opened gateways for us to expand as an end to end match box manufacturing company. This gives us a great edge over quality and timely delivery of products.

We have about 25 years of Export Profile. Some of the Countries in which we have done business are Yemen, Nigeria, Tanzania, Sudan

We also have a very good local profile within India. Our own Brands have penetrated the Indian market and positioned in market for its quality
